National Databank

Offers free mobile data for adults struggling to access the internet. You qualify if you are: 18+ years old AND from a low-income household AND/OR has no access or insufficient access to the internet at home AND/OR has no or insufficient access to the internet when away from the home AND/OR cannot afford their existing monthly contract or top-up.

MIT Emerging Talent

You are eligible to apply for a certificate in Computer and Data Science with MIT if you have a passion for technology and are eager to learn. This program is open to individuals from diverse backgrounds, including students, professionals, and lifelong learners. No prior experience in computer science or data science is required; however, basic proficiency in mathematics and analytical thinking is recommended. Participants will gain hands-on experience in coding, data analysis, and problem-solving, equipping them with valuable skills for the digital age.

Migration Scotland

Provides a comprehensive set of tools and resources for migrants, refugees, and asylum seekers in Scotland. These resources cover various aspects such as legal rights, healthcare, education, employment, and integration support, aiming to empower individuals and communities to navigate the challenges of migration and build successful lives in Scotland.

Afghan Human Rights Foundation

Offers practical support, help and advice on health, education, professional, and social services for Afghan refugees, migrant communities, and asylum seekers.

Best Start Grant & Best Start Foods

The Best Start Grant and Best Start Foods are Scottish Government schemes aimed at providing financial support to low-income families with young children. The grants help families cover the costs associated with pregnancy, childbirth, and early childhood, including expenses for baby essentials, such as clothing, food, and childcare. Eligible families can receive financial assistance to ensure the best possible start for their children.

Red Cross UK - Help for Refugees from Afghanistan

The British Red Cross offers support and assistance to refugees from Afghanistan who have resettled in the UK. Services include practical help with housing, healthcare, education, language learning, employment, and social integration. The Red Cross provides a welcoming and supportive environment to help Afghan refugees rebuild their lives and thrive in their new communities.

Scottish Refugee Council

A leading charity in Scotland dedicated to supporting refugees, asylum seekers, and vulnerable migrants. The Scottish Refugee Council provides a wide range of services, including advice, advocacy, education, and integration support. Through their work, they aim to uphold the rights and dignity of refugees and create a more inclusive society where everyone can live with respect and equality.

Additional Support in Scotland

Refugee Survival Trust (RST) offers additional support and assistance to refugees and asylum seekers in Scotland who may be facing specific challenges or hardships. This includes financial grants for essentials such as food, clothing, travel, and emergency accommodation. RST also provides advice, advocacy, and practical support to help individuals navigate complex asylum and immigration processes and access essential services.

Forth Valley Welcome Forth Valley Welcome is an organization dedicated to welcoming and supporting refugees and asylum seekers, particularly those from Afghanistan, in the Forth Valley region of Scotland. Their website features an introductory video available in both Pashto and Dari languages, providing essential information about the support services available and how individuals can access assistance. Through community outreach, advocacy, and practical support, Forth Valley Welcome aims to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for newcomers to the region.
